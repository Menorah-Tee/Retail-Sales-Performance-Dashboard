# Retail-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key insights on revenue, profit, product performance, regional sales, and customer demographics. An interactive Power BI dashboard was built to visualize sales trends and support data-driven business decisions.
[Download Excel File] (https://github.com/Menorah-Tee/Retail-Sales-Performance-Dashboard)

## Project Summary
Retail businesses rely on data to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and regional demand.
This Power BI dashboard analyzes retail sales data to uncover key insights into:
Revenue and profit performance
Product category performance
Regional sales distribution
Customer purchasing patterns
Monthly sales trends

## Executive Summary
The retail dataset recorded over 1,200 sales transactions, generating approximately ₦302M in total revenue and ₦60M in total profit.
Sales analysis shows that certain product categories and regions drive the majority of revenue, while customer demographics also influence purchasing patterns.
Key highlights include:
North region recorded the highest quantity sold
Electronics category generated the highest revenue
Male customers contributed the largest share of sales

## Industry Context
The Retail industry depends heavily on data-driven insights to improve:
Sales performance monitoring
Customer behavior understanding
Regional demand forecasting
Product inventory planning
Analytics dashboards help businesses make strategic decisions that improve revenue and profitability.

## Key Insights & Recommendations
## 1. Product Category Performance
Electronics generated the highest revenue, making it the most profitable category.
## Recommendation:
Increase inventory and marketing efforts around top-performing product categories.
## 2. Regional Sales Performance
The North region recorded the highest quantity sold, indicating strong market demand.
## -> Recommendation:
Focus marketing campaigns and distribution strategies in high-performing regions.
## 3. Customer Demographics
Male customers contribute the largest share of sales revenue.
## -> Recommendation:
Develop targeted marketing strategies based on customer demographics.
## 4. Monthly Revenue Trend
Sales fluctuate across months, showing periods of higher and lower demand.
## -> Recommendation:
Identify seasonal trends and align promotional campaigns accordingly.
